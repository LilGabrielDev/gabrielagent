# Conversations and Inbox

The Conversations page is your unified inbox where all customer interactions across WhatsApp, Email, Phone, and API are consolidated into a single view. This is where you monitor AI responses, intervene when needed, and manage the lifecycle of each conversation.

![Conversations](../screenshots/03-conversations.png)
*The unified inbox showing conversations across all channels with filtering, status indicators, and message previews.*

---

## Understanding the Unified Inbox

Every time a customer contacts your business through any channel, a conversation is created in Owly. The unified inbox displays all of these conversations in one place, regardless of which channel they originated from.

Each conversation in the list shows:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Customer Name | The name of the customer (or "Unknown" if not identified) |
| Channel Badge | An indicator showing whether the message came from WhatsApp, Email, Phone, or API |
| Message Preview | A snippet of the most recent message in the conversation |
| Timestamp | When the last message was sent or received |
| Status | The current state of the conversation (active, resolved, escalated, or closed) |

## Filtering Conversations

Use the filter controls at the top of the inbox to narrow down the conversation list.

### Filter by Channel

Select a specific channel to view only conversations from that source:
- **All** -- Show conversations from every channel
- **WhatsApp** -- Only WhatsApp conversations
- **Email** -- Only email conversations
- **Phone** -- Only phone conversations
- **API** -- Only conversations initiated through the REST API

### Filter by Status

Filter conversations based on their current lifecycle stage:
- **All** -- Show conversations in any status
- **Active** -- Ongoing conversations that need attention
- **Resolved** -- Conversations that have been addressed
- **Escalated** -- Conversations that were escalated to a human team member
- **Closed** -- Conversations that are finalized

### Search

Use the search bar to find conversations by customer name, contact information, or message content.

## Viewing Conversation Details

Click on any conversation to open the full message thread. The detail view shows:

### Message Thread

The complete history of messages exchanged in this conversation, including:
- **Customer messages** -- Messages sent by the customer
- **AI responses** -- Automated replies generated by Owly's AI
- **Admin messages** -- Messages sent by an administrator (you)
- **Tool calls** -- Actions the AI took during the conversation (such as creating a ticket or looking up customer history)

Messages are displayed in chronological order with timestamps.

### Conversation Metadata

On the side or top of the conversation, you can see:
- Customer name and contact information
- Channel the conversation is on
- Current status
- Satisfaction rating (if provided by the customer)
- Tags applied to the conversation
- Linked tickets

## Admin Takeover (Replying as Admin)

While the AI handles most conversations automatically, you can step in at any time to respond directly.

### How to Reply as Admin

1. Open a conversation from the inbox
2. Type your message in the reply input at the bottom
3. Send the message

Your message will be sent directly to the customer through the same channel. The AI will see your message in the conversation history, which helps it provide more relevant follow-up responses.

### When to Take Over

- The AI gives an incorrect or incomplete answer
- The customer requests to speak with a human
- The conversation involves a sensitive or complex issue
- A VIP customer contacts support

## Internal Notes

Internal notes are private annotations that you can add to a conversation. They are visible only to administrators and are never sent to the customer.

### Adding an Internal Note

1. Open a conversation
2. Switch to the notes tab or section
3. Type your note
4. Click **Add Note**

### Use Cases for Internal Notes

- Document context that the AI might not know ("This customer was promised a discount last week")
- Leave instructions for other team members ("Follow up on Monday about the replacement")
- Record decisions made during the conversation
- Flag conversations for review

## Customer Satisfaction Widget

Owly includes a satisfaction tracking system where customers can rate their support experience on a scale of 1 to 5 stars.

- Satisfaction ratings are stored per conversation
- You can view individual ratings in the conversation detail
- Aggregate satisfaction scores are available in the [Analytics](Analytics-and-Reports) page
- The satisfaction data helps you identify areas where the AI or your team can improve

## Conversation Status Lifecycle

Every conversation follows a defined lifecycle with these statuses:

```
active --> resolved --> closed
  |
  +--> escalated --> closed
```

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | The conversation is ongoing. The customer may still be waiting for a response or the issue is not yet resolved. |
| **Resolved** | The issue has been addressed. The conversation may be reopened if the customer follows up. |
| **Escalated** | The conversation has been flagged for human attention, typically because the AI could not resolve the issue. |
| **Closed** | The conversation is finalized. No further action is expected. |

### Changing Conversation Status

You can manually change a conversation's status from the conversation detail view. This is useful when:
- You have resolved an issue and want to mark it as done
- You need to escalate a conversation to a team member
- You want to close old conversations that are no longer active
